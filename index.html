About Us

At Cambridge Semantics, we take on and solve, the hardest challenges of making all data a reusable valuable asset and timely resource, no matter how big, fast or complex the data.

Our Anzo Smart Data Lake® solution, allows IT departments and their business users to semantically link, analyze and manage diverse data whether internal or external, structured or unstructured, with speed, at big data scale and at a fraction of the implementation costs of using traditional approaches.
